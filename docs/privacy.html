Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

Civica ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at civica.app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit the site, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Civica is a static website. We do not have a user accounts system, a database, or any server-side code that processes personal data. The information we may collect is limited to:

  • Analytics data (Google Analytics 4): If you have not opted out, GA4 collects standard web analytics data including approximate geographic location (country/region), browser type, device type, pages visited, and time on page. GA4 uses cookies. This data is aggregated and not linked to your identity.
  • Email address (Formspree): If you submit the "Get Notified" form, your email address is transmitted to Formspree, our form-processing partner, and stored in their system. We use this address only to send quarterly score-update notifications. We do not sell or share your email.
  • Browser localStorage: The "Compare" feature stores your selected town FIPS codes in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us.

2. Cookies

Google Analytics 4 sets cookies on your device to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. You can opt out of GA4 tracking at any time by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You may also clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Information

  • Analytics data is used solely to understand how people use the site (which town pages are most visited, what search terms bring people to the site) so we can improve it.
  • Email addresses are used only to send the quarterly score-update notification you signed up for. Each email includes an unsubscribe link.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties except:

  • Google Analytics: Analytics data is processed by Google under their Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree: Email form submissions are processed by Formspree under their Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare / Netlify (hosting): Our hosting provider processes request logs. These are retained per their standard data retention policies.

5. Data Retention

GA4 retains event-level data for 14 months by default. Email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. localStorage data is controlled entirely by you and your browser.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are located in the European Union or California, you may have additional rights including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights or to unsubscribe from email, contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Civica is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site evolves. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at privacy@civica.app.
