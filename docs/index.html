How the Civica Score works

One 0–100 score from five dimensions — about 58% resolved at the town level, the rest inherited from its county.

Affordability
Rent burden vs. town income, plus appreciation quality.
💼24 pts
Economy
Wages, sector quality, diversity, town income growth.
🛡️22 pts
Safety & Place
Town crime, scale, amenities, and physical risk.
📈15 pts
Growth
Town population growth, migration, and permits.
🎓14 pts
Schools
Public-school proficiency, ranked within the state.
